# ❤️ LoveDeck

### Truth or Dare for Couples

LoveDeck is a modern Truth or Dare game specially designed for couples who want to have fun, strengthen their relationship, and create unforgettable moments together.

Whether you're on a date night, in a long-distance relationship, newly dating, or already married, LoveDeck offers hundreds of romantic questions and exciting challenges that help couples connect on a deeper level.

---

## ✨ Features

💙 200+ Truth Cards  
Explore meaningful conversations, relationship reflections, funny confessions, future dreams, and deep emotional questions.

🔥 200+ Dare Cards  
Enjoy romantic, fun, and creative challenges designed to bring couples closer together.

🏆 Score System  
Complete challenges to earn points and compete to become the winner.

💕 Couple Focused  
Every card is carefully designed around love, relationships, communication, and connection.

🎨 Modern Design  
Clean, beautiful, and intuitive interface built with Flutter.

## ❤️ Why LoveDeck?

Most Truth or Dare games are made for groups of friends.

LoveDeck is different.

This game was created specifically for couples, helping partners:

- Start meaningful conversations
- Discover new things about each other
- Build emotional connections
- Create memorable date nights
- Have fun together anytime, anywhere

---

## 🎮 How To Play

1. Enter both player names
2. Choose the number of rounds
3. Pick Truth or Dare
4. Complete the challenge
5. Earn or lose points
6. The player with the highest score wins
